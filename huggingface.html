Limitations & Hardware Constraints

Known system boundaries and structural limitations to consider before deployment.

  • Language Mixing: The model can occasionally switch languages mid-sentence during reasoning.
  • Inference Compute: Hosting the full 671B model requires substantial GPU arrays (multiple A100/H100 nodes).
  • Formatting Repetitions: Can become verbose or repeat chain-of-thought blocks under edge prompts.
